Helping You Thrive in College

Lotus Mind is a mental wellness platform designed to support college students through the challenges of academic life and personal growth. Our goal is to make mental health resources accessible, easy to use, and tailored to your needs all in one place.

Aim

To develop a web and mobile platform that supports college students' mental health and well-being by providing access to counselling, self-assessment tools, resources, and peer support in a private and inclusive environment.

Objectives

  • Enable students to book counseling appointments anonymously.
  • Provide mood tracking and self-assessment tools for emotional awareness.
  • Offer a resource library with articles, videos, and motivational content.
  • Facilitate peer support through moderated community groups.
  • Provide emergency contact access and anonymous sharing of concerns.
